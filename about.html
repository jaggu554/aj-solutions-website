About AJ Solutions Private Limited

Reliable Back-Office & Data Processing Solutions

Who we are

AJ Solutions Private Limited is a Bangalore-based back-office support and data processing service provider delivering reliable and scalable outsourcing solutions. We specialize in handling high-volume non-voice processes with trained manpower, structured workflows, and strong quality control systems.

Our approach

We focus on accuracy, security and predictable delivery. Our teams use documented workflows and multi-level quality checks to ensure data integrity while meeting SLAs.

  • Work Capacity: 10,000 – 50,000 entries per day
  • High-speed internet connectivity & power backup
  • Dedicated supervisors and multi-level quality checks
